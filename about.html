About Me

The developer behind
the code

A curious mind, relentless builder, and firm believer that great software changes lives.

The Beginning

Curiosity sparked
a lifelong passion

Growing up in Ahmedabad, I dismantled every electronic device I could get my hands on — not out of destructiveness, but out of an insatiable need to understand how things worked. That same instinct carried me to computer science, where I discovered that software was the ultimate building material: infinitely malleable, globally scalable, and limited only by imagination.

My first "Hello World" at 14 set off a chain reaction. By 16, I was building school management tools. By 18, I was freelancing for local businesses. The journey from curious kid to professional developer felt less like a career choice and more like following gravity.

The Craft

Engineering with
precision & soul

I specialize in the full stack — not because I lack depth, but because I believe the best products are built by people who understand both the user's screen and the server's memory. I've led projects from whiteboard sketch to production deployment, touching every layer in between.

My technical philosophy is simple: write code that your future self (and your teammates) will thank you for. That means clean architecture, meaningful tests, thorough documentation, and a relentless commitment to performance and accessibility.

The Vision

Building toward
a better web

The internet is the most powerful communication medium humanity has ever created — and it deserves to be beautiful, fast, accessible, and trustworthy. Every project I take on is a small contribution toward that north star.

Beyond code, I'm deeply interested in design systems, open source, developer tooling, and the intersection of AI with creative engineering. I contribute to the community through open-source libraries, technical articles, and mentoring aspiring developers.
